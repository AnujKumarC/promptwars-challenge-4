# FIFA Nexus AI: Hackathon Pitch & Presentation Guide

This document outlines the Pitch Deck slides, Demo Script, and future expansion scope for **FIFA Nexus AI** to prepare for the PromptWars Hackathon submission.

---

## 1. The Hackathon Pitch (90-Second Script)

**Hook**: 
*"Imagine 80,000 screaming fans inside a World Cup stadium. Kickoff is in 30 minutes, but Gate C is jammed, a spectator in Section 104 is suffering a heat stroke, and a security alert is triggered near the media center. Traditional operations are blind, relying on delayed radios and spreadsheets. This is why we built FIFA Nexus AI."*

**Solution**:
*"FIFA Nexus AI is the neural Command Center for smart stadium operations. We connect fans, volunteers, security, medical, and admins into one unified, real-time platform. Under the hood, we leverage Google Gemini to translate and process voice queries in over 100 languages, alongside local spatial pathfinding to route fans around crowd bottleneck gates, manage triage patient lists, and audit green utility consumption dynamically."*

**Why It Wins**:
*"It's production-ready, default dark-mode premium, accessible with screen readers, and works fully offline. FIFA Nexus AI ensures a frictionless, sustainable, and safe tournament experience."*

## 3. Step-by-Step Live Demo Script

Follow this script to deliver a high-impact demo:

1. **Start on Landing Page**:
   - Show the interactive stadium graphic and live statistics. Explain that stats update dynamically.
2. **Access Command Center**:
   - Click **"Launch Command Center"**. Connect using email login.
   - Select the **"Fan"** role. Show the accessibility settings. Turn on **"Screen Reader Speech"** and toggle font scales to show layout responsiveness.
   - Search route path: select **"Entrance A"** to **"Seat Section 102"**. Select **"Wheelchair"** to highlight elevator bypass routes, then **"Low Crowd"** to bypass congested Gate C.
   - Open the **Gemini Floating Assistant** in the bottom right. Click **"Recommend Halal food"** to see responses, then order a *Halal Chicken Shawarma* and watch the preparation timer countdown.
3. **Switch to Volunteer View**:
   - Select **"Preview Volunteer"** from the top right role dropdown.
   - Show volunteer shift check-ins, tasks assigned, and complete a task to watch the **Performance Score** increment.
4. **Trigger Code Red (Admin)**:
   - Switch to **"Preview Admin"** to view command metrics and SVG analytics charts.
   - Tap **"Simulate Code Red"**.
   - Notice the system-wide warning alerts triggered across all views.
   - Open the **Map** and see it automatically switch to **Red Evacuation Mode**, drawing exit lines directly to *Emergency Exit East*.
   - Switch to **Security CCTV** to view simulated face logs, or **Medical View** to triage patients.
5. **Resolve Incident**:
   - Go back to Admin and click **"Resolve Emergency"** to restore normal stadium operations.

## 4. Future Scope & Roadmap

- **Augmented Reality Navigation**: Integrate WebXR AR guidelines so fans can view path directions directly through their phone cameras inside the concourse.
- **Physical IoT Beacon Tracking**: Use Bluetooth BLE beacons inside tickets to map high-precision crowd density values instead of camera estimation.
- **Predictive Queue Simulation**: Train custom ML models on ticketing scans to predict wait times 45 minutes before matches end, pre-allocating shuttle buses.
